Frequently Asked Questions

Can I mix services? (e.g., hourly + tasks)

Absolutely! I'm flexible with pricing. If you need a combination of monthly support + one‑off tasks, we can create a custom package. Just get in touch.

Do you offer a discount for annual payments?

Yes! Clients who pay for a 6 or 12‑month retainer upfront typically receive a 5–10% discount. Contact me to discuss.

What if my needs change mid-month?

No problem. Retainers can be adjusted with one month's notice. If you suddenly need extra hours one month, we can discuss a one‑off add-on.

Do you charge for initial consultations?

No! The first consultation is free. I'll ask about your needs and give you a quote with no obligation.

How do I pay?

I accept bank transfers, EFT, and can arrange payment plans for retainers. Invoice and payment details will be provided once we confirm your package.

What if I only need one student application done?

Perfect! You can start with a single service and expand later. Pricing is transparent and there's no lock-in contract for one‑off tasks.

Is there a minimum contract length?

For monthly retainers, I prefer a 3‑month commitment to build a good workflow. But we can discuss shorter trials if needed. One‑off tasks have no commitment.

Ready to get started?

Contact me for a free consultation. I'll help you choose the right plan and answer any questions.
